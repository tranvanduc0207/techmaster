Don’t Miss Out Maker Faire Shenzhen

Maker Faire Shenzhen showcases amazing projects of all kinds by makers of all ages — anyone who embraces the DIY do-it-yourself (or do-it-together) spirit and wants to share their accomplishments with an appreciative audience.

As the gateway for China’s “Reform and Opening up,” and as the testing ground for China’s Special Economic Zones, Shekou is the starting point for modern development of Shenzhen. This year’s Maker Faire Shenzhen will take place at Sea World, Shekou, taking people back to where it all started to feel the tempo and history of this city.
Event Date: October 21-23, 2016
Location: Sea World at Shekou, Shenzhen, China
Otto DIY booth number: B4-020
We look forward to seeing you in Shenzhen!
If is far for you just follow social media for the updates
