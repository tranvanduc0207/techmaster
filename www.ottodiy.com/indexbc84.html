      why is Otto special?

       

      Otto is truly Opensource; it means the hardware is easily discerned so that others can freely make it, compatible with Arduino, easy to 3D print and customize, and therefore,

      the perfect opportunity to build your very first robot, learn robotics and have fun.

       

      is more than just a robot toy; the act of building and coding your own Otto

      will create an emotional attachment between you and him. Otto brings children and people closer to technology. learn the logical connection between code and action, and by assembling it they understand how its components, and electronics work.

       

      Otto is the ideal companion for developing STEAM skills without gender gap. Via the instructions, children exercise mathematical thinking, reinforce their technological and physical knowledge and enhance abilities such as problem solving and creativity. Through play and experimentation children can learn about this technological product in a fun and educational way.

       

      Thingiverse Education (the biggest 3D printing community) certified that Otto with a 3D printer make teaching easier and more effective for a variety of grade levels and subjects. It also provides a community where educators can exchange best practices or remix projects.

    • scratch based coding; just drag and drop blocks

      easy to program with graphical and visual programming interface for beginners perfect for schools and workshops.

      quickly learn the principles of coding before jumping into C/C++ advanced command lines, algorithms and functions.

       

      programming and computational thinking

      in programming, finding a solution requires that a problem be divided into parts and that actions be sequenced; this helps to structure mind and thought, coding with Otto the children exercise their computational thinking and put skills into practice that are necessary for problem solving.

      one of the easy ways to code all Ottos is with Scratch based coding; an open software that allows easy visual programming with simple blocks and is available for the main OS.

      designed in APP inventor, our smartphone application allows control all moves, even create your own dance choreography for Otto DIY+ and Humanoid, as an alternative use our visual studio software to control from your computer.
